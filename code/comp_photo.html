CS194-26 Fall 2016 | Professor Alexei Alyosha Efros

The following are a series of computational photography projects I implemented throughout the course of fall 2016. Each focuses on a topic of interest and many involve implementing past research papers in the field. This was one of my most beloved college classes due to its merging of art and computer science, and the fact that it provided ample excuse for running around outdoors and shooting photos!

1. Coloring Grayscale Images

2. Camera Obscura

3. The Fascinating World of Frequencies

4. The Art of Seam Carving

5. Face Morphing

6. Playing with Light Field Data

7. Image Warping and Mosaic Construction

8. Tilt Shift Photography & Vertigo Shots
