Cocoon

Create a safe haven for your little caterpillar

Emin Arakelian, Michelle Chang, Fadi Kfoury, Neil Shankar, Aleksandrina Stoyanova

Contributions: Persona development, storyboards, and sketching; user research; branding, UI/UX design, videography

View on GitHub | Full report on design process

Cocoon is an ambient noise tracker that empowers pregnant women, particularly those in noisy, urban environments, to protect their developing babies from the harmful effects of sound. A dual Android Wear and mobile phone application, Cocoon features a real-time noise monitor which provides annotations to help users develop an intuition for what constitutes harmful sound levels. Cocoon also recommends safer, quieter locations to mothers in dangerously loud environments. The app allows users to view graphical visualizations of sound levels they have encountered, as well as offers information and resources for concerned mothers to keep themselves informed.
