Reach, Grow, Build (RGB): Event Cards

{ Spring 2016 }

RGB is an annual day of learning put on by InnoD that offers talks, workshops, and networking opportunities to students interested in design.

To advertise the event and give our attendees and speakers a little something to take home, I created a series of postcards to reflect the three core tenets of the event.
